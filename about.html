Meet Our Founder
Dr. Amanda Joseph, MD

Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Joseph is a board-certified psychiatrist with over seven years of clinical experience spanning hospital psychiatry, outpatient clinics, and private practice. After years of working in institutional settings, she saw the same problem over and over: patients were rushed through 10-minute appointments, handed a prescription, and sent on their way.

That's not care. That's a conveyor belt.

She founded Joseph Psychiatry to do things differently. Here, every patient gets the time they deserve ‚Äî thorough evaluations, individualized treatment plans, and a provider who actually listens. Her specialty is working with high-functioning adults who are dealing with ADHD, anxiety, and depression, often for the first time and often after years of being told "you're fine."

As Medical Director, Dr. Joseph designs and oversees every treatment protocol at the practice. She's built a team of experienced nurse practitioners and physician assistants who share her clinical philosophy: be thorough, be thoughtful, and treat every patient like a person, not a chart number.
